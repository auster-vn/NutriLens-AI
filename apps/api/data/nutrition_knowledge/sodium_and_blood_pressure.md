# Sodium and Salt

Sodium and salt are related but not identical. Product labels may show sodium or salt; salt is approximately sodium multiplied by 2.5.

For a low sodium goal, compare sodium per 100g/ml and watch processed snacks, instant noodles, sauces, and cured foods.
