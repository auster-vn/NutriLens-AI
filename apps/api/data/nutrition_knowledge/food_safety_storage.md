# Food Safety and Storage

Pantry tracking should distinguish pantry, fridge and freezer storage. Items close to expiry should be highlighted early so users can plan meals before food is wasted.

If a food smells unusual, packaging is damaged, or storage conditions were unsafe, expiry reminders alone are not enough to determine safety.
