# Reading Nutrition Labels

Use the nutrition label to compare products on a common basis, usually per 100g/ml and per serving. Focus on sugar, sodium, saturated fat, fiber, protein, calories, allergens, and serving size.

Marketing claims such as "natural", "light", or "healthy" should not replace the nutrition table. A product can look healthy while still being high in sugar or sodium.
